Frequently Asked Questions

Booking Tickets

1. How do I book movie tickets on Movie Magic?

To book tickets, simply browse our selection of movies, choose your preferred time and location, and follow the steps to complete your purchase.

2. Can I cancel or change my booking?

Bookings can be changed or cancelled up to 2 hours before the showtime. Please visit your account dashboard to make changes.

3. Are there discounts for bulk or group bookings?

Yes, we offer discounts for bulk bookings. Please contact our support team for more details on group discounts.

4. Can I view my booking history?

Yes, your booking history is available in your account dashboard under 'My Bookings'.

5. What should I do if I encounter issues during booking?

Contact our support team immediately via the Contact Us page, and we'll assist you.

Payments and Refunds

1. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and select digital wallets.

2. How secure are online transactions?

All transactions are encrypted with the latest security protocols to ensure your data is protected.

Promotion code and gift cards

1. How do I use a promotional code?

Enter your promotional code at checkout to apply the discount to your booking.

2. How do I use a e-giftcard?

Enter your e-giftcard number at checkout to apply the discount to your booking.

Others

1. Do I need to print my ticket?

No, you can show the digital ticket on your mobile device for entry.
